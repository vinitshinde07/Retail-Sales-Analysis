# Retail Sales Data Analysis & Customer Segmentation

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify business trends, top-performing products, profitable customer segments, and the impact of discounts on profitability.

The project was completed using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to simulate a real-world Data Analyst workflow.

---

## Business Problem

The objective was to answer key business questions:

* Which product categories generate the highest sales?
* Which categories generate the highest profit?
* Which regions contribute most to revenue?
* Which states drive the majority of sales?
* How do discounts impact profitability?
* What business recommendations can improve performance?

## SQL Analysis

The following business questions were analyzed:

1. Sales by Category
2. Profit by Category
3. Sales by Region
4. Top States by Sales
5. Discount Impact on Profitability

Key findings:

* Technology generated the highest sales.
* Technology generated the highest profit.
* Furniture produced strong sales but lower profit margins.
* California and New York were the highest revenue-generating states.
* Higher discounts were associated with lower profitability.
